Vegetarian Pizza

Description

Pizza is super easy to make. But have you ever made vegetarian pizza? It's just as easy, but requires a little more creativity in terms of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Cornmeal
  • Pizza sauce
  • Asiago, pecorino, mozzarella cheese
  • Roasted red peppers
  • Artichoke hearts
  • Red onion
  • Jalapeno pepper
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Prepare pizza dough
  2. Preheat oven to 200 degrees Celsius
  3. Mix together the three cheeses
  4. Spread sauce onto dough
  5. Add toppings
  6. Bake pizza for 10 to 13 minutes
