Vegetarian Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is super easy to make. But have you ever made vegetarian lasagna? It's just as easy, but requires a little more creativity in terms of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Fresh veggies
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Roast the veggies
  2. Boil the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta filling
