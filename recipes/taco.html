Vegetarian Tacos

Description

Tacos are super easy to make. But have you ever made vegetarian tacos? It's just as easy, but requires a little more creativity in terms of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Fresh veggies
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup crumbled Mexican cotija cheese
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped

Steps

  1. Saute the veggies
  2. Soften the tortillas
  3. Prepare the tortillas on the stovetop
  4. Add sauteed veggies to the tacos
